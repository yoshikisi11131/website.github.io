UBC AMS Block Party 2023

Annual Block Party at UBC ft. 24k Goldn, NGHTMRE, Armani White and more


On April 13, AMS hosted its annual Block Party, the largest university music festival in Western Canada, in the heart of UBC. As the school year was coming to a close, students were eager to grab a ticket to see acclaimed hip-hop and EDM artists. The Block Party featured 24kGoldn, Jauz, Nghtmre, Armani White, Daijo, Un!dentified, Crooksy and Wreckage, as well as many food trucks and activities throughout the event.
